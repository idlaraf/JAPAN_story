the japanese red-backed vole

this rodent lives only in the island of Honshu, he has asmall body and wheight normally 20g, they live near rivers or in a rocky habitat, in fact he escapes fastly from predators by hoping and jumping over the rocks using his agility.

japenese giant salamander

the japanese giant salamander is the second biggest amphibian in the world. About the size of a human, this species lives only in the southwestern japan, he eats fish, crabs,small mammals and frogs.

japanese wood pigeon

this bird live in the Ryukyu islands,this pigeon is bigger than the common pigeon and also the colours are different, he eats mainly insect and berries and get protected during winter with special feathers to keep the body warm.
